Eisenor is a manufacturer of linear guides with high rigidity and hardness similar to INA. Their guides are found in machine tools, automation, special construction, and in storage systems. Additionally, they produce the roller systems for the guides and can provide special thermal, chemical, and ceramic processes. They are specialist in custom guides such as curved.
