Grindel is a manufacturer of ground and lapped cylindrical gears in relatively small lot runs up to 1 m in diameter. They are suppliers to some major international companies as well as to most of the Spanish metal machine manufacturers. Around 80% of their product is exported, mostly to European countries. They also produce geared shafts up to 1 m in length and can provide ground internal teeth for cylindrical gears. Their quality level is between 5 and 8 depending on complexity and customer requirements.
